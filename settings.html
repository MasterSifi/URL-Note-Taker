Help

  1. Click on the blue icon adjacent to the date & time in the note area to open the link. Links open in a new page.
  2. When you disable the Save URL button, link is not saved. Thus, instead of a blue icon, you will see a grey icon that cannot be clicked.
  3. You can use the Download icon adjacent to the Settings icon to download your notes in a PDF file.
  4. Your notes stay even when you close the browser. You can save your notes and close the browser. The next time you open your browser, you can access your notes. Notes are removed only when you double-click the Delete All button.
  5. Click Delete All button twice to delete all the notes. You cannot retrieve your notes or links once you delete it. Therefore, download your notes before deleting it.
  6. The feature to delete individual notes is planned for future release.
  7. This application is 100% safe. No data leaves your browser. None of your notes are sent anywhere. All data are stored locally in your browser.
  8. Do not store sensitive information in the notes because data are stored in unencryped form.
  9. This extension does not require internet connection to work. However, when not connected, you can save notes only. Links can only be stored when you open a webpage, navigate to that page, and save note.
